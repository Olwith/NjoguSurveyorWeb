Why Choose Njogu Surveyor?

Registered & Trusted Surveyors in Kenya

With over a decade of experience, we are fully licensed by the Land Surveyors Board of Kenya and use the latest GNSS, Total Stations, drone technology and GIS solutions.

We combine traditional surveying expertise with modern technology to deliver precise, reliable results for every project — from small residential plots to large infrastructure developments.

  • Registered with Institution of Surveyors of Kenya (ISK)
  • Nationwide coverage — from Nairobi to all 47 counties
  • Fast turnaround & competitive pricing
  • 100% compliance with Kenyan survey regulations
  • Licensed drone operators for aerial surveys
  • Complete title processing & land transactions support
